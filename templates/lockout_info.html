Understanding our account lockout protections

Why Account Lockout?

To protect against brute-force attacks and credential stuffing, we implement an automatic account lockout policy. After multiple failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked.

Best Practices

  • Use a strong, unique password (min. 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, digit, and special character).
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available.
  • Never share your credentials with anyone.
  • Contact support if you're locked out unexpectedly.
  • Failed attempts reset after 15 minutes.
